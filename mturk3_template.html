Instructions
You must be using Google Chrome to do this study. Please do NOT do this study if you have recently cleared your browsing history, or if this is not the computer and web browser you use for your everyday browsing activity.
This is a research study in which we are gathering your browsing activity and browsing history over a period of 5 weeks, so that we can learn how much time you are spending on sites, how many tabs you keep open at once, and what you are doing at any given time. You just need to install our Chrome extension and keep it installed for 5 weeks (we will pay a bonus of $1 for each week you keep it installed and are actively using the browser).
(The mechanical turk terms of service permit HITs that require software so long as it is not harmful. The extension is open-source and the results will be shared with only the Stanford HCI Research Group.)
If you have any questions or issues, you can contact geza@cs.stanford.edu
